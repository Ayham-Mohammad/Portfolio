Riot Dungeon: Brand Banner Design

Project Overview

This project focused on creating a bold and dynamic brand banner for Riot Dungeon, a new Playstation gaming hub. The aim was to establish a strong visual identity that reflects the energy, excitement, and immersive atmosphere of gaming. The design was crafted to appeal to a young, modern audience by combining vibrant colors, striking typography, and a sense of adventure that captures the spirit of the brand.

My Process & Design Decisions

My design approach centered on building a bold yet visually balanced banner that would instantly grab attention. I chose a high-contrast, energetic color palette inspired by the intensity of competitive gaming and the immersive vibe of a dungeon-themed space. The typography was selected to feel modern and powerful, with a playful edge that connects to the excitement of the Playstation community. I kept the layout clean and structured so that the brand name remains the hero of the design, while still conveying the vibrant atmosphere that Riot Dungeon represents.
