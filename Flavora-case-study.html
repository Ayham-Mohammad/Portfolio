Flavora: Brand Banner Design

Project Overview

This project focused on creating a Funn and Hyped brand banner for Flavora, a new Waffle , crepe and cocktails shop. The aim was to establish a strong visual identity that reflects the energy, excitement, and fun atmosphere of sweets. The design was crafted to appeal to a young, modern audience by combining vibrant colors, excitement typography, and a sense of love that captures the spirit of the brand.

My Process & Design Decisions

My design approach focused on creating a warm and inviting banner that captures the delicious spirit of Flavora, a brand dedicated to waffles and crepes. I chose a rich, appetizing color palette inspired by golden waffles, melted chocolate, and fresh fruits â€” colors that instantly evoke sweetness and comfort. The typography was selected to feel modern yet friendly, reflecting the handcrafted, joyful nature of the brand. The layout was kept clean and airy to highlight the brand name as the centerpiece, while subtle design elements and textures convey the freshness, creativity, and indulgent atmosphere that define Flavora.
