Frola: Brand Banner Design

Project Overview

This project involved designing a brand banner for Frola, a new crepe, waffle, and cocktail shop. The primary goal was to create a vibrant visual identity that captured the fun and inviting nature of the brand to attract a young, modern audience.

My Process & Design Decisions

My approach focused on creating a clean, yet eye-catching design. I selected a color palette that reflected the fresh ingredients and energetic atmosphere of the shop. The typography was chosen to be both modern and playful, complementing the brand's unique offering. I aimed for a minimalist layout to ensure the brand name and delicious offerings stood out clearly.

Tools Used

  • Figma / Adobe XD
  • Adobe Photoshop
